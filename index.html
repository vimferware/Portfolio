About me

I’m Algenis Ferreira, a Full-Stack Java Developer based in New York, NY. I specialize in both front-end and back-end development, with a strong foundation in Java, JavaScript, React, and more. My goal is to leverage my technical skills to contribute to impactful projects and collaborate effectively with teams. Before transitioning into software development, I gained valuable experience in fast-paced culinary environments, which taught me the importance of organization, attention to detail, and teamwork. These skills have seamlessly translated into my approach to coding, where I prioritize delivering high-quality solutions. I’m actively working on various projects that allow me to apply my knowledge and grow as a developer, whether working independently or as part of a team. I’m eager to continue learning and contributing my skills in this exciting field. Outside of coding, I’m looking forward to one day learning to hang glide—it’s on my bucket list! Feel free to check out my work on GitHub and connect with me!
